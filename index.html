The Kingdom of Kintsugi

Kintsugi is a newly founded kingdom within the Stolen Lands a territory within the River Kingdoms. Kintsugi's capital is New Carth, located upon the northen shore of Tuskwater lake.

'Who claims this land - claims its pain and its death' — Mad Prophet

Kingdom Details

Capital

New Carth, the first settlement to be established in Kintsugi after the death of the previous regent the Stag Lord. The Lords of Kintsugi have established many politcal relations, with other settlements or kingdoms with the likes of Varnhold, Restov, and Tatzlford. Most of which choosing to remain allies, but the latter Tatzlford electing to become a freeholf within Kintsugi. The Lords of Kintsugi also gained the devotion of the Lizardfolk in the southern shore of Tuskwater Lake, whoose village would come to be known as Lizardberg. Most recently a Oleg close friend and ally of the lords, established his own settlement within Kintsui's borders known as Olegton.

Settlements

  • New Carth
  • Tatzlford
  • Lizardberg
  • Olegton
