Leaders of Kintsugi

In the Kingdom of Kintsugi, each character plays a vital role in shaping the realm's destiny. Below are details about each individual and which role they play:

Bartemeus

Kineticist -- Viceroy

A anceint skeleton kineticist, Bartemeus refered to ocassionally as "Boney Bart", used to be a Genie hailing from Carth, but he was enslaved and forced to posses the skeleton of his captor after his death to survive.

Fumbus

Alchemist -- Emissary

A dangerous goblin alchemist, Fumbus much like his elven ally prefers the use of fire and explosions to vanquish his enemies, he can often be found drinking and partying with Grub when not on adventures.

Grub

Fighter -- General

A stalwart human fighter and devout follower of Cayden Cailean, Grub enjoys to spend his time drinking and carousing about the kingdom when he's not crushing the skulls of his foes with his trusty warhammer or getting parts of himself crushed by said foes.

Hadriel

Wizard -- Magister

A pretentious elf wizard, Hadriel often chooses to cast fireball first and ask questions later, he chooses to put a great deal of distance between himself and his adversaries. This habit also extends to his allies, choosing to conjure his personal cabin in lieu of the camping accomadatons the rest of the party has.

JR-ZY

Gunslinger -- Ruler

An automaton gunslinger, designation JR-ZY or "Jersey", provides intense firepower from afar, wielding a variety of firearms, most favorably his Barricade Buster when the situation calls.

Jubilost (Companion)

Alchemist -- Treasurer

See Companions [Jubilost]

Neva

Cleric -- Counselor

A homely half-hrc cleric of Cayden Cailean, Neva Eldastrong is the backbone of the party offering magical healing when needed, and often taking up the role of cook when out on adventures.

Samuel

Barbarian -- Warden

A brutal half-orc barbarian, Samuel Krag a bounty hunter by trade initially joined the party, who would found Kintsugi, after a close encounter with a troll. Samuel wields the rage of a red dragon, channeling draonic might into his attacks to scorch those who would bring harm to the citzenry of Kintsugi.
