Roles in the Kingdom of Kintsugi

In the Kingdom of Kintsugi, each leader plays a vital role in shaping the realm's destiny. Below are the roles with key abilities and their responsibilities:

Counselor

Key Ability: Culture

Serving as the kingdom’s moral compass and public voice, the Counselor bridges the gap between ruler and citizen. They listen to the people’s concerns, interpret their will, and translate policies into actions the populace understands and accepts. A skilled Counselor builds trust and ensures that the government leads with empathy as well as wisdom.

Emissary

Key Ability: Loyalty

Part diplomat, part spymaster, the Emissary deals in secrets, alliances, and subtle influence. Whether acting openly as a high-ranking envoy or operating in the shadows, they shape both internal politics and foreign relations. Their hands may be clean—or stained—but their actions secure the kingdom’s interests in a dangerous world.

General

Key Ability: Stability

The General commands the kingdom’s military forces and oversees their readiness in peace and prowess in war. They lead armies, manage garrisons, and ensure that the kingdom's borders are defended with strength and discipline. Their decisions can win wars—or prevent them entirely.

Magister

Key Ability: Culture

The Magister governs the kingdom’s magical affairs, from spellcasting schools to arcane infrastructure. They balance the realm's mystical forces, ensure that magical knowledge is used wisely, and help integrate supernatural resources into everyday governance. In a world shaped by magic, their guidance keeps both wonders and terrors in check.

Ruler

Key Ability: Loyalty

The Ruler is the sovereign heart of the kingdom — the final word in law, diplomacy, and governance. They preside over major state ceremonies, sign laws into being, pardon criminals, and appoint all major officials. Their leadership sets the tone for the entire realm, from the battlefield to the marketplace, and their vision becomes the kingdom’s destiny.

Treasurer

Key Ability: Economy

Guardian of the realm’s purse, the Treasurer oversees trade, taxation, and fiscal policy. They ensure markets remain fair, industries thrive, and corruption is rooted out. A savvy Treasurer keeps the economy flowing, investments growing, and confidence in the kingdom’s future strong.

Viceroy

Key Ability: Economy

The Viceroy is the architect of the kingdom’s expansion and infrastructure. They chart new territory, plan settlement growth, build roads and bridges, and develop connections between cities and the wilds. Their work ensures the kingdom’s lifeblood — travel, trade, and communication — remains strong and resilient.

Warden

Key Ability: Stability

Tasked with safeguarding the kingdom’s people and lands, the Warden leads border patrols, defends against regional threats, and keeps order in untamed territories. They act as a watchful guardian over rural areas and ensure that the reach of the law extends beyond the city walls. The Warden is the kingdom’s shield against chaos.
